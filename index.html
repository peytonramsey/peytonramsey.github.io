Causality

Airbnb: US Market Classifier & Paris Investment Study

Built a national CatBoost classifier on 374K US listings (Kaggle AUC 0.8668), then retrained it on Popincourt, Paris to answer an investor question: does gentrification create a booking advantage? The answer is host type creates a booking advantage. Casual hosts book at 44% vs. 11.5% for commercial operators, a gap that price, amenities, and geography can't catch up with.

0.966 holdout AUC

On AI

Working with generative models through my research role has sharpened one view: AI is most effective when treated as a skill, not a shortcut. It can genuinely expand your thinking and accelerate work, but it can just as easily anchor you to mediocre results if you're not skeptical. The practitioners who get the most from it understand its capabilities and its failure modes equally well.
