Predictive Modeling

Airbnb: US Market Classifier & Paris Investment Study

What drives conversion, and can host operating scale explain booking success beyond price alone?

Built a CatBoost model on 374K U.S. Airbnb listings, then extended the analysis to a 93K-row Paris panel to study what separates high-conversion listings from the broader market.

Engineered 25+ features, ran 5-fold stratified cross-validation, and tuned the model with 40 Bayesian optimization trials to improve predictive performance and isolate meaningful business signals.

Achieved 0.966 holdout AUC versus a 0.8668 national baseline, and found host operating scale remained an important conversion signal even after controlling for multiple price-related features.

Credit Risk · MLOps

Loan Default Prediction with Drift Monitoring

Can a credit risk model stay useful after deployment, not just perform well at training time?

Built a Random Forest pipeline on 148K loan applications to predict default risk using feature engineering across 51 borrower and loan variables.

Added PSI drift monitoring, MLflow tracking, Dockerized deployment, and a live inference interface so the model could be evaluated as an operational system rather than a one-off experiment.

Achieved 0.92 ROC-AUC and paired predictive performance with monitoring for emerging model risk.

Time Series · Anomaly Detection

Macroeconomic Anomaly Detection

Can multivariate macroeconomic behavior be used to detect economic shifts before they are obvious?

Built an LSTM autoencoder on 7 FRED indicators using 12-month windows to detect abnormal co-movement through reconstruction error.

Grounded the design in VECM cointegration analysis, calibrated the anomaly threshold on a pre-2007 baseline, and deployed the system with MLflow tracking and forward-looking explanations.

Correctly identified all 3 major demand regime shifts in the study period: the 2008 financial crisis, COVID-19, and the 2022 Fed hiking cycle.
